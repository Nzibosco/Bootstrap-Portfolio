About me

My name is Bosco. I like playing games on my PC. It makes me feel I am on top of the world! I am learning how to make fun games to help people have fun too. The world has become crazy and chaotic! We need some free time to play.

In the end, that is what makes life more fullfilling. Not boring! Finally, I like to wish people joy and success. It makes me happy when y'all happy.

This is my awesome quote from Stewart B. Johnston: "Our business in life is not to get ahead of others but to get ahead of ourselves-to break our own records,
to outstrip our yesterdays by our today, to do our work with more force than ever before."
